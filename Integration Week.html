My integration week has been an exciting experience, meeting people form different parts of central africa, and discovering a rich academic experience. Here, I'll take you through my thoughts on campus life,the facilities, the people and the opportunities that await. Ucac-Icam's campus is designed to foster both academic and personal development. Here are some of the highlights that stood out of me:

  1. The Chapel:

    2. In the campus the is a chapel. A peaceful space for reflection and prayer, crucial for a catholic university like Ucac-Icam, where spirituality is central to the student experience.

  2. Bureau Des Etudiants(BDE):

    3. The BDE plays a central role in students interest.We have an active student council, planning events and activities that help new students like me feel more at home.

  3. Sport Facilities:

    4. There are dedicated spaces for basketball, football, and even a swimmingg pool! these areas offer a break from academics and an opportunity to engage in physical activities and socialize. whether you're an athlete or not, the sport facilities cater to all skill level and encourages students to stay active and balance.

  4. Study and Workspaces:

    5. Students have access to modern workspaces, perfect for collaboration and focused study, which is important in our engineering courses.

  5. Dormitories

    6. Comfortable and well-equipped dormitories, offering students a safe and conducive environmentent to study and relax. The dorms foster a sense of community and provide an opportunity for students to interact and create lasting friendships

  6. The Refectory:

    7. The campus refectory is alively space where students gather for meals.Offering a wide variety of dishes, ensuring that all students are well catered for. It's more than just a place to eat; It's a social hub where students share stories over breakfats and lunch.

  7. Healthcare:

    8. Ucac-Icam institute takes students health seriously, offering health care facilities that provides quick access to medical attention. It provides assured medical services, adding a layer of security for students. With dedicated team of health professionals, students can easily get medical consultations and support, ensuring well-being throughout their academic journey

  8. Club and Social

    9. The is no shortage of extracurricular activities at Ucac-Icam. From academic and cultural clubs, students can pursue their passions and interests outside the classroom. These clubs provide a platform for creativity, collaboration, and personal development, ensuring a well-rounded student experience.

Meet Father Maximilien BOSSOU :

During our intergration week at Ucac-Icam, we were introduced to father Maximielien BOSSOU, responsible for the management of the IT course at the institute. Father Max, as he is affectionately called, plays a pivotal role in shaping the educational journey of students at the university. He encourges us to blend faith and engineering, emphasizing the Jesuit values that Ucac-Icam hols hear. He frequently reminds us that being an engineer is also about acquiring knowledge responsible to serve others. His words inspire a deep sense of purpose and community among us as we embark on our studies.

As a catholique institution run by Jesuits, Ucac-Icac fosters an education that goes beyond academic excellence. The values of Technical Competence, Human Responsability, and Spiritual growth are deeply ingrained in the student experience. I was impressed by how much emphasis is placed on not only becomming a highly skilled engineer but also on developing into a responsible , ethical, and well-rounded individual. I'll look forward to the comming months of learning, personal growth, and making a meaningful impact both inside and outside the classroom.
